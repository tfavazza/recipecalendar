20-22 June - Ricotta Cookies (Felicia)

Cookies
- 1 cup softened butter
- 2 cup sugar
- 2 large eggs, beaten
- 1 lb ricotta
- 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda

Icing
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon crisco
- 4   cup 10X sugar
- 1   teaspoon lemon juice

Cookies
Preheat oven to 350F. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla and continue to beat at medium speed. Add ricotta and beat until mixed well. Mix dry ingredients together and add to mix a little at a time. Dough will be very thick. Beat another 30 seconds. On parchment paper scoop small round balls and bake 10-12 minutes until edge of cookies are golden brown. Cool. 

Icing
Warm milk and add butter and crisco until melted. Whisk 10X sugar and lemon juice until smooth.
