03 June - Creamy Rice Pudding

- 3   cups whole milk
- 1   cup heavy cream
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup risotto rice 

Bring liquids and sugar to a boil. Add risotto and simmer 30-40 minutes, stirring often. When adding rice add vanilla bean, orange, or lemon rind or any flavor you want. When rice is done add 2/4 teaspoon almond extract. Sprinkle with sugar and put under boiler to caramelize.
