09 June - Oven Chicken Piccata

- chicken breasts
- 6 lemon slices
- 1/2 cup lemon juice
- chopped garlic
- 1/4 cup parsely 
- 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
- 3 tablespoons capers
- salt, pepper

Preheat oven to 425F. Line pan with foil and coat with cooking spray. Place chicken in pan, combine juice, garlic, parsely, sauce, salt and pepper. Pour over chicken. Bake about 49 minutes. Transfer chicken to platter, Add capers to pan and pour over chicken. Serve with lemon wedges.
