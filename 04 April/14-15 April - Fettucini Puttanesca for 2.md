14-15 April - Fettucini Puttanesca for 2

- 6 oz fresh fettucini
- 2 tablespoon sliced green olives
- 1 tablespoon fresh chopped garlic
- 1 pinch of pepper
- 1 pinch of hot pepper
- 2 cups plum tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon capers
- 1 oz or 6 anchovies in oil
- 2 oz olive oil

Fry at medium heat all of the above ingredients except the plum tomatoes. When the full aroma of the garlic and anchovies is present, add plum tomatoes and break down tomatoes. Again, simmer slowly. IN the meantime boil fresh pasta for 3-5 minutes. WHen pasta is done, then Puttanesca is done. Adding touch of sherry wine to sauce is suggested.
