01 April - JeJe's Italian Cookies

- 4   cup flour
- 1   cup Crisco
- 1   cup sugar
- 4   eggs
- 4   teaspoon baking powder
- 4   teaspoon vanilla
- 1/3 cup milk

Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder and crisco in a bowl. Will be crumbly. Beat eggs, vanilla, and milk together until frothy, and add to flour mixture. Bake at 350F until light brown, about 15 minutes. 

Flatten some dough about 4" wide and 6" long. Fill with lemon, close like a turnover.
