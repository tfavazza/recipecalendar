16-17 April - Chicken Tarragon for 2

- 12-14 oz boneless chicken breast
- 1/2   cup freshley sliced mushrooms
- 6     slices fresh thinly slied tomatoes
- 3     oz chicken broth
- 1/2   teaspoon dried tarragon
- pepper 
- fresh parsley
- 2     oz white wine
- butter for frying

In butter blanch both sides of the chicken on high heat. Add chicken broth and wine, mushrooms, tomato, and tarragon. Work everything in together. Simmer, turning chicken over once or twice. This will all blend and thicken
