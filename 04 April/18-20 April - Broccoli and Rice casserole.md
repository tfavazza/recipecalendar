18-20 April - Broccoli and Rice casserole

- 1/2 cup chopped celery
- 1 chopped onion
- 3 tablespoon margarine or butter
- 1 box frozen chopped broccoli
- 1 can cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
- 1/2 can of milk
- 8 oz cheese whiz
- 2 cup cooked rice

Saute celery and onions in margarine, add frozen broccoli and stir until thawed. Add soup, milk and cheese whiz. Blend all ingredients until cheese is melted. Fold in cooked rice, place in a greased casserole dish, cover. Heat in 350F oven for 30 minutes.
