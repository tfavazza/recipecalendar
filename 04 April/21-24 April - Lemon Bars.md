21-24 April - Lemon Bars

- 3/4   cup (1 1/2 stick) margarine or butter, softened
- 3/4   cup sugar
- 1     egg
- 2     teaspoon vanilla
- 2 1/4 cup flour
- 1     cup (1/2 22 oz can) lemon pie filling
- confectioners sugar

Preheat oven to 375F. In large mixing bowl with electric mixer beat margarine, sugar, egg, and vanilla until well blended. Mix in flour until incorporated. Divide dough in half and shape each into a log about 14" long and 1 1/2" wide. Place on greased baking sheet using the handle of a wooden spoon or your finger make a deporession down the center of each log about 1/2" wide. Spoon in lemon filling, mounding slightly. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until tops are set and bottoms are golden. Let cool two minutes and loosen bottoms with spatula. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar.
