02 April - Coffee Cake

- 1   Frozen dough
- 1   stick melted butter
- 1   package vanilla pudding
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup pecans or walnuts

Put frozen dough in a well buttered bundt pan. Pour melted butter over dough. Combine sugar, pudding and nuts and sprinkle over dough. Cover and let rise overnight. Bake for 30 minutes at 350F.
