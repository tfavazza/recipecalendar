16 February - Grace's S Cookies
<pre>
- 4   eggs
- 4   teaspoon vanilla
- 1   stick butter
- 1/2 cup Crisco
- 1   cup sugar
- 4   cup flour
- 4   teaspoon baking powder
</pre>
Glaze
<pre>
- 1   cup confectioners sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract
- 1/4 cup milk to proper consistency
</pre>
Beat eggs on 4 for 4 minutes. Add vanilla while still beating. Pour eggs into smaller bowl. Cream crisco and butter with sugar using mixer or whisk. Mix flour and baking powder. Alternate adding eggs and flour to sugar mixture in mixer (use dough hook). Scrape with spatula as needed. Take out, form ball, should feel velvety. Turn bowl over it and let rest for 10 minutes. Cut dough with dough measure 6" roll to breadth of larger than pinky. 375 10 min for the first batch and 8 minutes for rest. 

<i>(Editor's note: I was told to bake 4 minutes on bottom rack and 4 minutes on top rack of oven).</i>
