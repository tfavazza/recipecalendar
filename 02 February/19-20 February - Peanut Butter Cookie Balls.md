19-20 February Peanut Butter Cookie Balls

Cookie dough
<pre>- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2   teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2   cup unsweetened cocoa 
- 1/2   cup sugar
- 1/2   cup margarine or butter
- 1/2   cup brown sugar
- 1/4   cup peanut butter
- 1     teaspoon vanilla
- 1     egg
</pre>
Filling
<pre>- 3/4 cup peanut butter
- 3/4 cup confectioners sugar
</pre>
Topping
<pre>- melted chocolate</pre>

In a small bowl combine flour, cocoa, and baking soda. In a large bowl beat sugar, brown sugar, margarine and 1/4 cup peanut butter. Beat until light and fluffy. Add vanilla and egg and beat in well. Stir in flour mixture and blend. Set aside. Combine filling mixture and blend. Roll about 30 balls with mixture. Flour hand and roll cookie dough around balls. Bake at 375F for 7-9 minutes. Cover with melted chocolate.
