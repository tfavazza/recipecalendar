14-15 February Orange Freeze Pie

<pre>- 1   baked pie crust
- 3   oz package orange gelatin
- 2/3 cup boiling orange juice
- 1   cup vanilla ice cream, softened
- 11  oz can of mandarin oranges, drained
- 1   cup La Creme whipped topping, thawed (Cool Whip)
</pre>
Dissolve gelatin in boiling orange juice. Add ice cream by spoonfuls, blending until dissolved. Chill until slightly thickened. Stir in mandarin oranges. Fold in LaCreme. Pour into baked pie crust. Freeze until firm, about 2 hours. Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serving. Serves 8.
