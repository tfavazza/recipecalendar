03-04 February - Pizza / Bread Dough (Geri Nicastro)
<pre>
- 2     cup lukewarm water
- 4 1/2 teaspoons dry yeast  (2 packages)
- 1/2   stick melted margarine or butter 
- 2     tablespoon sugar
- 1     tablespoon salt
- 5     cup flour
</pre>

Mix all together and knead until smooth and elastic. Makes 2 big pizzas/
