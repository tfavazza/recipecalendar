07-08 February - Eggplant Relish Cappalotina <i>(Editor's Note: Caponata?)</i>

<pre>- eggplants
- onions
- celery
- green olives
- capers
- tomato paste
- vinegar 
- sugar
</pre>
Cut eggplants cube size, fry until cooked - drain oil. 

Fry onions and celery and when almost done add olives and capers. REmove from frying pan and using the same oil cook tomato paste - add vinegar. If too tart add a little sugar to taste and simmer for a few minutes. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
