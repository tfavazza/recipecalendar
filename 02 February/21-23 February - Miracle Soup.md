21-23 February - Miracle Soup 

<i>(Editor's note: This seemed more like a crash diet plan than a real "recipe", but I kept it in here anyway.</i>
<pre>
- 6 large onions
- 2 green peppers
- 1 large cabbage
- 1 large bunch celery
- Whole tomatoes, canned or fresh
- Season with onion soup mix or other mix
</pre>
Cut vegetables in medium size pieces, cover with water and bring to a boil, simmer until soft. May be taken anytime any day. 

DIET
<pre>
Day 1: Fruits except bananas. Melons preferred 
Day 2: Vegetables - no peas, corn, or dried beans. 1 large potato with butter
Day 3: Fruits and vegetables - no potatoes
Day 4: Bananas (6) skim milk (8 cups), soup
Day 5: Beef - 10-12 oz, tomatoes (6), 8 glasses of water
Day 6: Beef - all you want - vegetables no potato
Day 7: Brown rice - fruit juice - vegetables. 
</pre>
