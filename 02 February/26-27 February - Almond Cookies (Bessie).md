26-27 February - Almond Cookies (Bessie)
<pre>
- 2 lb baked almonds
- 6 cup flour
- 1 lb sugar
- 1 lb brown sugar 
- 4 tablespoons baking powder
- 8 eggs beaten
- 2 tablespoons cinnamon + Italian C
- 2 teaspoon clove
- 2 tablespoon orange zest</pre>

Add hot almonds and mix altogether. Put oil on hands to handle dough easier. Roll in strips and bake at 300F for 25 minutes.
