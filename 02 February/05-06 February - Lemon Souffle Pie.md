05-06 February - Lemon Souffle Pie
<pre>
- 3   eggs separated
- 3   additional egg yolks
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2   teaspoon grated lemon zest
- 3   tablespoon lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
- 1   pie crust
</pre>

Preheat oven to 350F and cook pie crust for 10 minutes. Cool completely. 

In a medium stainless steel bowl set over simmering water whisk all 6 yolks until foamy. Whisk in sugar, lemon zest and juice, whisking constantly until thickened - about 8 minutes. Remove from heat and whisk for 1 minute more. Set aside

Beat the three egg whites until glossy and firm. Fold egg whites in yolk mixture until blended. Pour filling gently into pie crust and bake 20 minutes or until puffed and golden. Cool awhile serve warm.
