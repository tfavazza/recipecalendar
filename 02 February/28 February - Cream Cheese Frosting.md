28 February - Cream Cheese Frosting
<pre>
- 3   oz cream cheese, softened
- 1/4 cup butter, softened
- 1   tablespoon vanilla
- 2   cup sifted powdered sugar (10X)
</pre>
In a medium bowl with electric mixer, combine cream cheese, butter and vanilla - beat until fluffy. Add 10X sugar graudally and beat until smooth and creamy
