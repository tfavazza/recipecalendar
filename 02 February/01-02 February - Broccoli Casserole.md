01-02 February - Broccoli Casserole 
<pre>
- 30    oz frozen broccoli spears
- 1 1/2 can of cream of chicken soup
- 2     tablespoon flour
- 1 1/2 cup sour cream (use 3/4 of a 16oz container)
- 1     cup grated carrots
- 2     tablespoon grated or chopped onion
- 1/2   teaspoon salt
- 4     tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 cup corn bread or stuffing mix
</pre>

Cook broccoli according to package directions and drain. While broccoli cools, mix in a bowl the soup and flour, then add sour cream, carrots, onion, salt, pepper, and blend together. Cut up broccoli and place in pan greased with cooking spray. Pour soup and sour cream mixture over top of broccoli and work it through with spoon. Melt butter in pan, add stuffing mix together and pour over top of casserole. Bake 350F for 30 minutes.
