29 October - Cranberry Orange Salad

- 1   orange
- 7   oz can whole cranberry sauce
- 3   oz package of any berry gelatin or orange
- 3/4 cup water

Wash unpeeled orange, cut in quarters and remove seeds. Put orange quarters through food chopper. Stir cranberry sauce to soften. Dissolve gelatin in hot water and fold in cranberry mixture. Chill about 3 hours and serve with salad greens.
