05-06 October - Popovers

- 1   cup sifted flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1   cup milk
- 1   tablespoon oil
- 2   eggs

Heat oven to 425F. Grease muffin pans. Measure all ingredients into bowl and beat until very smooth. Should be like heavy cream. Fill cups half full and bake about 35 minutes. They should bake until sides and top are well browned and crisp. Do not open over door while popovers are baking.
