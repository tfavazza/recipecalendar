01 October - Zucchini Bread (Aunty)

- 3     cup flour
- 2     teaspoon baking powder
- 1     teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2   teaspoon salt
- 1/2   teaspoon cinnamon 
- 3     cup sugar
- 4     eggs
- 3     cups zucchini
- 1 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 1     cup chopped nuts

Mix together dry ingredients, then add the rest. Bake in a preheated oven 350F in a greased and floured loaf pan for about 1 hour. Test for doneness and cook longer if necessary.
