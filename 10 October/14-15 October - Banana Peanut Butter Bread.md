14-15 October - Banana Peanut Butter Bread

- 3     medium bananas (1 cup)
- 1     tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/4   cup softened butter or margarine
- 1/4   cup chunky peanut butter
- 1/2   cup sugar
- 1     egg
- 1/3   cup milk
- 1/2   teaspoon salt
- 1 3/4 cup flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2   teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4   cup unsalted peanuts

Heat oven to 350F. Grease 9x5x3" loaf pan. In small bowl mix mashed bananas with lemon juice. Mix all ingredients except peanuts until blended and smooth. Then stir in peanuts. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan, bake 50-55 minutes until toothpick comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes then remove to rack to cool completely before slicing.
