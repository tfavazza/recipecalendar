02 October - Pineapple Zucchini Bread

- 3     eggs
- 1 3/4 cup sugar
- 1     cup oil
- 2     teaspoon vanilla
- 2     cup grated zucchini, skins and all
- 3     cup flour
- 1     teaspoon baking powder
- 1     teaspoon baking soda
- 1     teaspoon salt
- 1     cup crushed pineapple, drained
- 1     cup chopped nuts, optional

Beat eggs, sugar, oil and vanilla together until fluffy. Add the rest of the ingredients. Grease and flour 2 loaf pans. Bake at 350F in a preheated oven for 1 hour. May take long - test for doneness.
