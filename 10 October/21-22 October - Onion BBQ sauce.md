21-22 October - Onion BBQ sauce

- 1/2 cup butter or margarine
- 1   tablespoon flour
- 2/3 cup ketchup 
- 1   cup finely chopped onions
- 2   envelopes G. Washington's Seasoning (Editor's note: this seems to be bouillon?)
- 1   tablespoon vinegar
- 2   tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
- 1   cup water
- 1   tablespoon sugar

Melt butter in large skillet, add onions and cook until tender. Stir in remaining ingredients and cook gently for 30 minutes. Baste any type of meat with sauce while BBQing.
