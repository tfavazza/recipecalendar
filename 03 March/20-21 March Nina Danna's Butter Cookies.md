20-21 March Nina Danna's Butter Cookies

- 6     cup flour
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 2     teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2   teaspoon baking soda
- 1     cup butter
- 3     eggs
- 1/2   cup orange juice

Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time alternate with orange juice. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda together and add to wet ingredients. Brush cookies with 1 beaten  egg and teaspoon of milk. Add sesame seeds. Bake for 10-15 minutes in a preheated 400F oven.
