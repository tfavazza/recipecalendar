04-05 March Basil Pesto Sauce (Emily Grillo)
<pre>
- 2   cup packed cut up basil
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1/2 cup grated cheese
- 1   teaspoon salt (optional)
- 1/2 cup pine nuts
- 2-3 cloves of garlic
</pre>
Basil-Parsley Pesto
<pre>- 1   cup parsley
- 3/4 cup grated cheese
- 1   teaspoon salt (optional)
- 2   tablespoon basil
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2-3 cloves of garlic
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 2   tablespoon boiling water
- 2   tablespoon butter
</pre>
For both recipes - put all ingredients in blender until smooth. Walnuts may be used instead of pine nuts.
