02-03 March - Cream Cheese Clouds
<pre>
- 8   oz softened cream cheese
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar (10X)
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1   cup heavy cream
- 1   can cherry pie filling
-     chopped nuts (optional)
</pre>
Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with electric mixer. Gradually add cream and mix well. Whip until thickened. Using the back of a spoon shape into 10 3 1/2" shells. Place on wax paper lined cookie sheet. Freeze at least 2 hours or overnight. Fill with cherries etc.
