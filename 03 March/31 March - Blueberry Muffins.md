31 March - Blueberry Muffins

- 1     cup blueberries
- 1/2   cup butter, melted
- 1 3/4 cup flour
- 2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4   teaspoon salt
- 1/2   cup sugar
- 1     egg
- 1/2   cup milk

Heat oven 375-400F. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar with butter. Add egg and milk and beat until smooth. Stir in blueberries. Top with sugar cinnamon.
