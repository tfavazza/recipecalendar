18-19 March - Cole Slaw (Cathy from Conn)

- 1   large head of cabbage
- 2   cup chopped green peppers
- 2   Spanish onions
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1   teaspoon dry mustard
- 1   cup white vinegar
- 1   teaspoon salt
- 1   teaspoon celery seed
- 3/4 cup vegetable oil

In a small saucepan add sugar, salt, oil, and vinegar. bring to a boil. Add to cabbage mixture. 
Cabbage should equal about 2 1/2 quarts(?)
