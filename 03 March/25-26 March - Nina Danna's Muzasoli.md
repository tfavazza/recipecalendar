25-26 March - Nina Danna's Muzasoli (Editor's Note: Uncle Stephen says Grandma said this is the one to make)

- 6 cups flour
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon clove
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 3 eggs
- 1 pint dark molasses 
- sesame seeds
- raisins (optional)

Mix all dry ingredients together. Add shortening and work into flour mix. Add eggs and molasses. Raisins are optional. Roll and spread on sesame seeds. Place on greased cookie sheet and bake in a 375F preheated oven for 8-10 minutes
