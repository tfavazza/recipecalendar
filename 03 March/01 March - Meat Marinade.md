01 March - Meat Marinade
<pre>
-     Olive oil, enough to coat the meat
-     Balsamic vinegar or wine(use lemon juice if chicken or lamb), about 1/3 the amount of oil
- 1   tablespoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon pepper
- 1-2 clove garlic, finely minced
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1   teaspoon oregano
- 2   teaspoon finely chopped parsley
</pre>
For pork
<pre>
- 1 tablespoon of molasses or brown sugar
</pre>
Add the following if you have it
<pre>
- Chopped basil
- Chopped mint (especially with lamb)
- Chopped sage or any herb you like
</pre>
Place all in a place bag big enough to hold the meat you want to marinate. Combine ingredients and add meat to it. Close the bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, longer is better.
