25-26 December - Chicken and Rice

- Chicken
- Italian dressing
- 1 bag of frozen mixed vegetables
- 1 cup water
- 1/3 cup of cooked rice
- Parmesan cheese

Saute chicken in Italian dressing. Add package of frozen mixed vegetables and water. Bring to a boil, Stir in cooked rice and set aside for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
