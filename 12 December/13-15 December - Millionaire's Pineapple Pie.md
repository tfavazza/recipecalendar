13-15 December - Millionaire's Pineapple Pie

- 3   egg whites
- 1   cup sugar
- 1   teaspoon vanilla
- 21  Ritz crackers, crushed
- 2/3 cup crushed walnuts
- 16  oz cream cheese
- 3/4 cup confectioners sugar
- 1   pint heavy cream 
- 8   oz crushed pineapple, drained
-     extra nuts (optional)

Beat egg whites until very stiff add sugar and vanilla. Fold in cracker crumbs and crushed nuts. Bake in a non-stick 10" pan in a 350F oven for 30 minutes. Cool. Blend cheese, confectioners sugar and spread over cooled meringue evenly. Combine whipped and sweetened, add drained pineapple fold in. Spread over cheese filling. Sprinkle with nuts. Refrigerate overnight. To serve cut in small squares and place in cupcake paper cups.
