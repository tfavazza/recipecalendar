11-12 December - Walnut Cheese Log (Connie Kennefich)

Walnut Cheese Log

- 1     lb finely grated cheddar cheese
- 8     oz package cream cheese
- 1/4   cup soft butter
- 1     teaspoon seasoned salt
- 1 1/2 cup chopped nuts
- 2     tablespoon pimento
- 2     tablespoon ripe olives

Beat cheeses, butter, and salt together until smooth. Stir in half of the chopped nuts all the pimento and ripe olives. Chill mixture for easy handling. Shape into log or ball, roll in remaining nuts. Chill until firm and serve with crackers.
