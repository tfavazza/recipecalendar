16-17 December - Balsamella Sauce

Use as filling for lasagna instead of ricotta

- 6   tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3   cup hot milk
-     white pepper
-     grated nutmeg
- 1   crushed bay leaf
- 1/2 teaspoon thyme

Melt butter over medium heat, blend in flour, salt and stir. When mixture foams pour milk all at once and whisk rapidly. Season with pepper and nutmeg. Drop in a bouquet garni of bay leaf and thyme and stir over lowest flame for 5 minutes. Stir occasionally. Remove garni.
