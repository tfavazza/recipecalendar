25-26 July - Grace Davis Cookies

- 1/2 box keebler graham crackers
- 1/2 cup crushed nuts
- 1 stick butter
- 1 stick margarine
- 3/4 cup sugar

Bring butter, margarine and sugar to a boil and let boil 3 minutes. Stir in nuts. Crackers come in fours. Break into individual pieces, cover cookie sheet completely and pour mixture over crackers. Bake in a 350F preheated oven for 10 minutes. Lay on wax paper and sprinkle with powdered sugar, let cool. Taste better if refrigerated.
