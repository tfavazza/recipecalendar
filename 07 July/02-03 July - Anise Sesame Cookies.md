02-03 July - Anise Sesame (cookies?)
<pre>
- 6     cup flour
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 6     teaspoon baking powder 
- 1 1/2 cup crisco
- 6     eggs
- 1/2   teaspoon vanilla
- 1     teaspoon anise
-       sesame seeds
-       milk
</pre>

Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, and crisco by hand until coarse meal. Add eggs, vanilla and anise. Form dough and let sit for 10 minutes. Form balls of dough and dip in milk, then in sesame seeds. Bake in preheated 425F oven for 10-12 minutes on a greased cookie sheet. 

Note:
To this recipe add cherries, nuts, and chocolate chips - 1 cup each. Do not add anise, just vanilla, spoon onto cookie sheet.

<i>Editors Note: I was able to 1/6th this recipe with no ill effects, makes 10-12 cookies. </i>
