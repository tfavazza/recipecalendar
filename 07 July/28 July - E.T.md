28 July - E.T. Bars

- 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
- 1 lb confectioners sugar
- 1 cup melted butter or margarine
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 12 oz chocolate bits

Grease a 9x12" pan. Mix all ingredeients except chocolate bits. Spread into pan evenly. Melt chocolate bits and spread over mixture. Refrigerate until set.
