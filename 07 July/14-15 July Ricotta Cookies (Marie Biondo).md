14-15 July Ricotta Cookies (Marie Biondo)

Blanche Magne (blancmange?)
- 10 oz milk, scalded
- 1 lemon peel
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 heaping tablespoon cornstarch
- 2-3 cinnamon sticks

Dough
- 4 cup flour
- 6 teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4-1 cup sugar
- 1 cup crisco
- 4 eggs beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

Filling
- blancmange
- 2 lb ricotta
- milk as needed
- honey to taste
- sweet chocolate, slivered

Blancmange
Remove lemon peel and cinnamon sticks before mix thickens. This should be refrigerated overnight. Sieve through ricer when cooled. 

Dough
Mix dry ingredients, add crisco, then eggs and vanilla which were beaten together. Add milk if needed. 

Filling
Mix blancmange and other filling ingredients together. Bake in preheated 375F oven on greased cookie sheets for about 10 minutes.
