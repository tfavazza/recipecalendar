23-25 August - Jennie's Dark Brown Sugar Cookies

- 1   cup dark brown sugar
- 3   cup flour
- 1/2 teaspon cloves
- 2   cup almonds, chopped
- 1   cup sugar
- 2   teaspoon cinnamon
- 2   teaspoon baking powder
- 3   eggs
- 2   tablespoon water
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil 

glaze
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 teaspoon water

Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl. Mix wet ingredients together and add to dry to form a dough. Separate dought into 4 balls, roll out about 9" long and flatten with fingers. Beat egg yolk and water and glaze dough before baking. Bake in greased cookie sheet in a preheated 375F oven for 25-30 minutes. Cut at an angle when cooled.
