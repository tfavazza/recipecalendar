01 August - Italian Cookie Plain or Filled

- 4 cup flour
- 4 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup crisco
- 4 eggs beaten with a little milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 4 teaspoon baking powder

Mix altogether and shape any way or fill with your favorite filling (lemon, fig, apple butter etc). Bake on a greased cookie sheet in a preheated oven 375F for 12-15 minutes.
