30 August - Ricotta Puffs

1 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla
2 eggs
1 lb ricotta cheese
2 1/2 cup vegetable oil
powdered sugar

Heat oil in a saucepan to 375F. Mix all ingredients except powdered sugar. When well mixed add a tablespoon of mix to hot oil. Within a minute balls will form puffs, turn and cook until golden brown. Drain on a paper towel and sprinkle with powdered sugar. Makes 2 dozen.
