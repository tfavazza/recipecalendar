19 August - Crescent Filled Cookies (cream cheese)

- 2 1/2 cup flour
- 2 sticks butter
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1 egg yolk

Mix altogether and store in refrigerator for 24 hours. Make a circle with dough and cut in pie shape wedges. Use flour and sugar to roll out dough. Bake at 375 about 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
