16-18 August - Crescent Filled Cookies (soured cream) (lotta work)

- 4 cup flour
- 1 lb margarine/butter
- 1 pint sour cream
- 2 egg yolks
- sugar/cinnamon/jelly

In a bowl put flour, margarine/butter and mix together. In another bowl mix sour cream and egg yolks. Add the mixture together and refrigerate for at least 24 hours. Roll dough on surface covered with flour and sugar (Dough will be very soft). Make a cirlce and cut into pie wedges shape. Fill with sugar, cinnamon, fruit-jelly etc. Do not grease pan. Bake in a preheated 400F oven until golden brown, 15-20 minutes.
