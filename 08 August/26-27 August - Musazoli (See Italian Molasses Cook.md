26-27 August - Musazoli (See Italian Molasses Cookie recipe)

4 lbs flour (Editor's Note: I think this is actually cups)
1 tablespoon ginger
1 cup crisco
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cup sugar
1 quart molassses
2 tablespoon black pepper
8 teaspoon baking powder
2 eggs

Mix flour cirsco baking soda, baking powder. In a separate bowl mix eggs, molasses. Add ginger, cinnamon, sugar, pepper to the flour mix. Add this mixture to molasses mix and mix well. If too sticky, add more flour. Bake at 400F preheated oven on a greased cookie sheet for about 10 minutes. Shape any way!
