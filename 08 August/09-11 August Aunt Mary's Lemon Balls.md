09-11 August - Aunt Mary's Lemon Balls

- 6   eggs
- 1/2 cup oil
- 10  teaspoon baking powder
- 1   teaspoon lemon extract
- 1   cup sugar
- 5   cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Beat eggs until fluffy, add sugar oil and extracts. Add all dry ingredients. You can shape them into balls. Bake in a preheated 375F oven on a greased cookie sheet for 10 minutes. After they have cooled, ice tehm with powdered sugar and water and lemon extract.
