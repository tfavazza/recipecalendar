07-08 Magic Cookie Bars

- 1/2   cup melted butter
- 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
- 1     cup coconut
- 1     cup walnuts, chopped
- 12    oz semisweet chocolate bits
- 14    oz condensed milk

Preheat oven to 350F. Pour melted butter into a 13x9" glass baking dish sprinkle cracker crumbs evenly over melted butter then sprinkle coconut and chocolate bits. Pour condensed milk evenly over all the ingredients in dish. Top with nuts. DO NOT STIR!! Bake for 35-40 minutes. Cool completely and cut into squares.
