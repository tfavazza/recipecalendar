26-27 September - Oatmeal Bread

- 1 1/2 cup boiling water
- 2     teaspoon salt
- 1     cup rolled oats
- 1     package yeast
- 3/4   cup molasses
- 2     cup lukewarm water
- 3     tablespoon soft butter
- 8     cup flour

Pour boiling water over oats and let stand 1/2 hour. Add molasses, butter and salt. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water and add to oat mix. Add flour until dough is medium soft and knead about 10 minutes. Put dough in a buttered bowl and let rise about 1 hour. Knead again and divide in two.
