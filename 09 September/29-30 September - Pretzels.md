29-30 September - Pretzels

- 1     package yeast
- 1 1/2 cup warm water
- 1     teaspoon salt
- 1     tablespoon sugar
- 1     egg
- 4     cup flour
-       coarse salt

Mix yeast, water, sugar, salt. Stir in flour and make a dough. Knead until smooth. Shape pretzels, put on cookie sheet. Use a Teflon one or one greased with oil. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse salt. Bake in a preheated oven at 425F for 12-15 minutes or until brown.
