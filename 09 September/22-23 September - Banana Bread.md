22-23 September - Banana Bread

- 1 3/4 cup flour
- 2/3   cup sugar
- 2     teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4   teaspoon baking soda
- 2     eggs
- 1/2   teaspoon salt
- 1/3   cup shortening
- 1     cup mashed bananas (2-3 ripe bananas)

Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together. Set aside. Cream shortening, sugar, and eggs until light and fluffy. Alternate flour mixture with mashed bananas to creamed mixture. Pour into well greased loaf pan and bake in a preheated 350F oven. Bake 1 hour 10 minutes. Ovens vary, so check after 50 minutes.
