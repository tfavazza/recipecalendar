15-16 September - Apple Kuchen

- 1     yellow cake mix
- 1/2   cup flaked coconut
- 1     stick butter
- 2 1/2 cup sliced apples
- 1/2   cup sugar
- 1     teaspoon cinnamon
- 1     cup sour cream
- 1     egg

Mix together cake mix, coconut, and butter until crumbly and put in a 9x13" ungreased pan and bake for 10 minutes. Place sliced apples on top and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Combine sour cream and egg and dribble on top. Bake in a 350F oven for another 25 minutes. You can substitute plain yogurt for sour cream.
