11 January - Pasta Frolla (Home Style Biscotti)
<pre>
- 3 1/4 cup flour
- 2/3   cup sugar
- 1/4   teaspoon salt
- 1     teaspoon baking powder
- 12    tablespoon cold unsalted butter
- 3     large eggs
- 1     orange's worth of grated zest
- 2     teaspoon vanilla
</pre>

(Can be filled with any jam or lemon)

Combine dry ingredients, stir well to mix. Rub in the butter, leaving mixture cool and powdery, not pasty. Whisk eggs with orange zest and vanilla ad stir into the flour mixture to form a dough. Knead lightly on a floured surface, folding dough over itself 3 or 4 times. Wrap in plastic and chill until firm, about an hour. Divide dough into 3 pieces, roll each into cylinder shape. Flatten, fill with jam, using less than 3 tablespoons for each roll. Bring edges together to seal. Bake 350F about 20 minutes. Frost lightly.
