07-08 January - Cannoli Dough (Grace Marino)
<pre>
- 3   cup flour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1   teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon salt 
- 3   tablespoon shortening
- 2   eggs 
- 2   tablespoon white vinegar
- 2   tablespoon water
</pre>

Mix together dry ingredients. Cut in shortening, and beat eggs together separately and add into the mixture. Finally, blend in the vinegar and water. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and knead. Wrap in wax paper and chill in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. For best results refrigerate for at least 2+ hours before using.
