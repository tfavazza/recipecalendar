12-13 January - Hard Almond Cookies (Bessie)
<pre>
- 2   lb whole almonds, heated in a 350F oven for 15 minutes
- 6   cup flour
- 1   lb brown sugar
- 1   lb sugar
- 2   tablespoon baking powder
- 8   eggs, well beaten
- 2-3 cinnamon sticks
- 2   tablespoon grated cinnamon 
- 2   tablespoon cloves
- 1   orange's grated zest 
-     vegetable oil
</pre>

Mix everything but almonds together. Add hot almonds and mix again. Use oil to make dough more workable if it is too sticky. Spray table with cooking spray to make cake loaves. Cut at an angle after cooked.
