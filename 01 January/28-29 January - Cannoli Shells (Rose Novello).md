28-29 January - Cannoli Shells (Rose Novello)
<pre>
- 1   cup flour
- 1   tablespoon sugar
- 1   tablespoon butter
- 1/4 cup white wine
</pre>

Place flour in a bowl or food processor. Add the butter and sugar and mix until mealy. Slowly add the wine and form the mixture into a workable ball. You may need to add a little more wine if dough is too dry. Knead the dough until smooth. Refrigerate, covered until ready to use.
