16-17 January - Italian Chocolate Ball Cookies
<pre>
- 1   box Devil Food Cake Mix
- 2   cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon cloves
- 1   tablespoon cinnamon
- 2   eggs
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1/2 cup nuts
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1   teaspoon baking powder
</pre>

Mix all together and shape into medium balls. If dough is too soft add more flour. Grease cookie pan and bake 12-15 minutes at 375F. Frost or dust with confectioners sugar.
