26-27 January - Carrots Elegante
<pre>
- 1 lb carrots thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup golden raisins
- 1/4 cup butter
- 3   tablespoon honey
- 1   tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 cup sliced almonds
</pre>

Cook carrots covered in 1/2" boiling water for 8 minutes and drain. Turn carrots into a 1 quart baking dish. Stir in raisins, butter, honey, lemon juice and ginger. Bake uncovered in a preheated 375F oven for 35 minutes, stirring occasionally. Spoon into serving bowl and sprinkle with almonds.
