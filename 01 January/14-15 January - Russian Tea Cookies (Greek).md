14-15 January - Russian Tea Cookies (Greek)
<pre>
- 1     lb margarine or butter
- 1 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
- 2     teaspoon vanilla
- 4 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2   teaspoon salt
- 1/2   cup chopped nuts 
</pre>

Cream together butter, sugar, and vanilla. Sift dry ingredients together and stir into the butter mixture. Bake at 400F for 10-12 minutes.
