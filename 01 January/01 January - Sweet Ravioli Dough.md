01 January - Sweet Ravioli Dough
<pre>
- 3   cup flour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup Crisco
- 3/4 cup milk
- 1   lb lard
- 1/4 cup oil
- sugar/cinnamon mixture
</pre>

Mix all ingredients until a smooth dough forms. Roll out with rolling pin medium thin. Fill dough with ricotta or chickpea filling (see January 2nd and 3rd), and use small turnover press to form ravioli and use fork to close edges.
In a large frying pan add lard and oil. Drop turnovers carefully and brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels. While still warm, dip and roll in sugar and cinnamon mix.
