30-31 January - Christmas Cherry Nut Gift Cake
<pre>
- 8     oz cream cheese, softened
- 1     cup butter or margarine, softened
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 4     eggs
- 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 1/4 cup flour
- 1/2   cup chopped walnuts
- 8     oz jar red cherries, chopped
- 1/2   cup finely chopped walnuts
</pre>

Thoroughly blend cream cheese, butter, sugar and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Gradually add 2 cups of the flour and baking powder. Combine remaining flour with cherries and 1/2 cup of chopped nuts. Fold into batter. Grease a 10" bundt pan then sprinkle with the 1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts. Pour batter into pan and put into preheated 325F oven for 1 hour and 20 minutes. Cool, remove from pan and decorate with green and red cherries, walnuts to correspond with the Christmas season.
