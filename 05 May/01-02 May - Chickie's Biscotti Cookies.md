01-02 May - Chickie's Biscotti Cookies 

- 6     cup flour
- 2 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 2     cup sugar
- 6     eggs
- 1     cup margarine or butter
- 1     package of toasted almonds
- 2     teaspoon anise extract
Optional 
- 1/2 cup mixed fruit
- 1/2 cup cut up cherries 

Mix all dry ingredients together. Mix in margarine. Add fruit and nuts (almonds). Mix eggs with anise and beat. Add to flour mix. Preheat oven to 400F. Bake 15-18 minutes. Remove from oven, slice, turn and brown each side for 5 minutes. Make loaves then slice at an angle.
