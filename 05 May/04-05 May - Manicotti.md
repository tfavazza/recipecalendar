04-05 May - Manicotti

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup water
- 2 eggs
- pinch of salt

Mix all ingredients and fry on grill which is lightly greased until batter is dry. Batter will not brown - flip to reverse side and fry until dry. Store in layers of wax paper.

Manicotti Filling

- 2 lbs Ricotta 
- 2 eggs
Season to taste with
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley 
- grating cheese

Cover bottom of pan with sauce. Fill manicotti with filling, making sure the open end is on the bottom. Cover with more tomato sauce. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
