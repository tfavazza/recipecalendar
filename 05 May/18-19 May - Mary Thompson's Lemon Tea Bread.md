18-19 May - Mary Thompson's Lemon Tea Bread

- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 6     tablespoon butter, margarine, or oil
- 1     cup sugar
- 2     eggs
- 1     teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2   teaspoon salt
- 1/2   teaspon lemon or vanilla extract
- 1/2   cup milk
- 1/2   cup nuts
- 1     fresh lemon (grate the rind)

Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and beat well. Add dry ingredients alternately with milk. Add nuts, lemon rind, and extract. Preheat oven to 350F and put batter in a well greased 3x5 loaf pan. Bake 35-45 minutes. Cool 5 minutes. Mix together the juice from the lemon and 1/2 cup sugar. Pour over bread while in pan and let stand 30 minutes before turning out of pan.
