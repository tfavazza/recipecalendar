17-18 November - Quiches

- Pastry for 2 crust pie
- 3/4 chopped meat
- 1/4 cup sliced onion
- 4   oz shredded Swiss cheese
- 1/3 cup mayonnaise 
- 2   eggs
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

Roll pastry and cover pie. Fill with all other ingredients which were mixed together in a bowl. Bake in a preheated oven set at 400F for about 25-35 minutes or until golden brown. You can make individual ones by using muffin pans and fill and bake for 15-20 minutes.
