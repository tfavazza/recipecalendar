10 November - Chocolate Satin Frosting

- 2   cup sifted confectioners sugar
- 1/4 cup soft butter
- 1   tablespoon milk
- 1   teaspoon vanilla
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1   egg
- 2   oz Nestle's choco-bake (editor's note: this doesn't exist anymore, but you can use 6 tablespoons cocoa powder and 2 tablespoon shortening instead)

In a bowl combine sugar, butter, milk, vanilla, and salt. Beat until creamy; beat in egg and blend in choco bake. Mix well. Makes about 2 cup frosting.
