05-06 November - Fried Ricotta Ravioli

Dough
- 2   cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2/3 cup Crisco
-     ice water as needed

Filling
- 2 lb Ricotta cheese
- 1 egg
-   powdered sugar
-   slivered chocolate
-   cinnamon

Dough
Mix together like pie crust. 

Filling
Combine all ingredients. 

Fill ravioli as macaroni and fry in hot oil. Drain on paper towels. May be dipped in a cinnamon sugar mix or shake with powdered sugar.
