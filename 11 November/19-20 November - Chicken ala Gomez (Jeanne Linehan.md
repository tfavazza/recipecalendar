19-20 November - Chicken ala Gomez (Jeanne Linehan)

Marinade
- 1-2 cut chicken (editor note: chicken cutlets?)
- 4   oz white wine
- 4   garlic cloves, mashed with salt
-     oregano
-     bay leaf
-     parsley

Marinate above for several hours. 

Dish
- 3   stalks celery, chopped
- 5   wedges of tomatoes
- 1   sliced onion
- 3   sliced potatoes
- 2   sliced carrots
- 2-3 teaspoon olive oil
- 6   oz white wine

Place chicken in baking pan, and cover with vegetables. Pour olive oil and white wine over top. Bake at 350F for 1 hour, adding more wine if needed. Fish may be substituted for chicken.
